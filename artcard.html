ABOUT THE ILLUSTRATOR

Hello, I am Jelornie Jarrett, a Jamaican-based Computer Science student at the University of Technology, Jamaica, and a character illustrator operating under the alias OP._Ecstasy.

My work focuses on character illustration, fan art, poster composition, and digital painting, with a strong interest in expressive storytelling, dynamic posing, and stylized rendering. Drawing inspiration from anime, video games, movies, and contemporary digital artists, I aim to create illustrations that feel both visually striking and memorable.

Outside of art, I am actively developing my skills in software engineering and web development, combining my interests in technology and creativity through projects such as this portfolio website. My goal is to continue growing as both an artist and developer while building experiences that blend visual design with interactive technology.
